# Pricing hypothesis (draft)

> This is a **pricing hypothesis**, not an active billing contract.

Torqa 0.1.6 is still validating first-user value. This page proposes packaging tiers for interviews, pilots, and launch messaging.

## Tier hypothesis

### Free / OSS

- CLI (`torqa`) and local scan flow
- Basic JSON/HTML report generation
- Local developer usage and CI experiments

### Team

- Dashboard access for shared scan history
- Share links and report collaboration
- Scheduled scans and alert routing
- Workspace-level policy templates

### Enterprise

- SSO / enterprise identity integration
- Audit logs and compliance export depth
- Custom policy packs and approval controls
- Hosted engine options / managed operations

## Test assumptions

- Teams will pay for recurring governance workflows, not single-run scans.
- Shared visibility (history + links + alerts) is the first strong paid value boundary.
- Enterprise demand will center on identity, auditability, and policy customization.

## Validation prompts

- Which workflow/risk events require real-time team notification?
- What evidence is needed for internal audit or customer assurance?
- Which policy controls are mandatory vs. nice-to-have?
- Is hosted execution preferred over self-host for your team size?
